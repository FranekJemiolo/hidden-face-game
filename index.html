🎮 How to Play

Controls

  • Drag horizontally: Rotate Y-axis layer (rows)
  • Drag vertically: Rotate X-axis layer (columns)
  • Arrow keys: Rotate camera around the cube

Goal

Return the cube to its solved state by rotating slices.

Colors

The cube uses 6 colors (light gray instead of white for better text contrast):

  • ⬜ Light gray - Top face
  • ⬛ Yellow - Bottom face
  • 🟥 Red - Front face
  • 🟧 Orange - Back face
  • 🟩 Green - Right face
  • 🟦 Blue - Left face

Inner pieces are dark gray. When scrambled, colors will be mixed - restore the original pattern to solve!

Numbers

Each tile shows its unique ID (0-26). These numbers help track individual pieces as they move through the cube. In the solved state, tiles are arranged in order.

Sharing

Click "Share Puzzle" to copy a URL that lets others play the exact same puzzle with the same seed and scramble.

Tips

  • Use arrow keys to see different faces of the cube
  • Drag slowly to see which slice will rotate (highlighted in green)
  • The arrow indicator shows rotation direction during drag
  • The same puzzle can always be solved by reversing the scramble moves
